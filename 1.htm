Our Objectives
Founded on 21st Jan.1973 by revered Madhav Sadashiv Golwalkar and run by Vidya Bharati. Gita Niketan Awasiya Vidyalaya aims at providing quality education based on Indian moral values to the students. Besides, having made manifold progress since its inception, the school enjoys the reputation of being a prestigious institution in North India.
